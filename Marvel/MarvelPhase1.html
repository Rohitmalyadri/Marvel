MCU Phase 1 Overview: Phase 1 of the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) consisted of six superhero films released between 2008 and 2012. It began with Iron Man in May 2008 and concluded with The Avengers in May 2012. source
Key Characters: Phase 1 introduced iconic characters like Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, Hulk, Black Widow, and Hawkeye. These characters formed the core team of the Avengers, who assembled to fight against Loki and his alien army in The Avengers.
