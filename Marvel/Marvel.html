About Marvel:

The Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) is a media franchise created by Marvel Studios, primarily set in a shared universe with some installments in adjacent or branching realities. It features characters that have appeared in Marvel Comics publications. source
Popular MCU Characters: The MCU includes some of the most popular superheroes, including Spider-Man, Captain America, Iron Man, Thor, Hulk, Black Widow, and many more. These characters have appeared in numerous films, television series, and other media. source
MCU Films and TV Shows: The MCU has released 30 films and several television series, with more planned for the future. These films and shows have been met with critical and commercial success, grossing billions of dollars worldwide.
