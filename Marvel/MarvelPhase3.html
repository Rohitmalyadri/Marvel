About:

Marvel Cinematic Universe Phase 3: Phase 3 of the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) spanned from 2016 to 2019 and included 11 films. It began with Captain America: Civil War and concluded with Spider-Man: Far From Home. The phase also featured the highly anticipated crossover films Avengers: Infinity War and Avengers: Endgame.
Key Characters Phase 3: introduced several new characters to the MCU, including Doctor Strange, Black Panther, and Captain Marvel. It also saw the continued development of established characters like Iron Man, Captain America, and Thor.
